Background and Significance of the study

ㅤㅤThe major focus of our project is SADAKAB's financial activities. In a recent interview, the institute admitted that money is mostly their problem. This means
ㅤㅤthat they struggle to make payments and purchase supplies for their community. The project's goal is to publicize and set up a contribution page for SAKADAB,
ㅤㅤwhich will enable the institution to raise money for supplies and bills and enable the community to grow across the nation. We may assist SAKADAB with its
ㅤㅤproblems with promotion and setting up beneficial finances for the community; through this, the project can successfully support SADAKAB as a whole.
